Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our coffee, shipping, subscriptions, and farm operations.

How fresh is your coffee when it arrives?

+

Our coffee is roasted to order and shipped within 48 hours of roasting. We use special packaging with one-way valves to preserve freshness during shipping. Most customers receive their coffee within 5-7 days of roasting, ensuring optimal flavor and aroma.

Do you offer international shipping?

+

Yes! We ship to over 25 countries worldwide. International shipping typically takes 7-14 days depending on your location. Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on weight and destination. All international orders include proper customs documentation.

Can I modify or cancel my subscription?

+

Absolutely! You can modify, pause, or cancel your subscription at any time through your customer portal. Changes made before your next scheduled shipment will take effect immediately. There are no cancellation fees or long-term commitments.

What makes Honduran coffee special?

+

Honduran coffee benefits from ideal growing conditions: high altitude, volcanic soil, and perfect climate. Our six distinct regions each produce unique flavor profiles. Additionally, our commitment to sustainable farming and direct trade relationships ensures exceptional quality while supporting farming communities.

Do you offer wholesale pricing for coffee shops?

+

Yes, we welcome partnerships with coffee shops, restaurants, and retailers. Our wholesale program offers competitive pricing, consistent quality, and reliable supply. Minimum order quantities apply. Contact us directly to discuss your specific needs and receive our wholesale pricing guide.

What is your return policy?

+

We stand behind the quality of our coffee. If you're not completely satisfied, contact us within 30 days of purchase and we'll make it right. This might include a replacement, refund, or store credit depending on the situation. Your satisfaction is our priority.
